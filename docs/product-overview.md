# iBOX

---

## For Guides and New Users

iBOX is a cloud-based business management system designed to streamline key business processes, including sales, purchasing, logistics, and financial operations.

The platform helps businesses manage daily operations in a single system and provides tools for working with products, warehouses, customers, suppliers, and payments.

---

## For Integrators and Partners

iBOX is a cloud-based platform that helps businesses automate workflows related to purchasing, inventory management, sales, payments, and reporting.

The system supports:
- multicurrency operations
- role-based access control
- integrations with external services
- API-based extensions (upon request)

iBOX replaces disconnected business tools with a single integrated solution where all key processes are connected.

This reduces risks related to manual data entry, eliminates duplicated information, and improves operational visibility.

For integrators and partners, iBOX provides:
- flexible configuration options
- scalable architecture
- support for different business models and workflows

## For Wiki / Knowledge Base

iBOX is a modular cloud-based ERP system designed to manage core business processes for small and medium-sized businesses — from purchasing and warehouse operations to sales, finance, and analytics.

The platform adapts to different business types and scales, including:
- retail
- wholesale distribution
- manufacturing
- service-based businesses

iBOX consists of configurable modules, allowing companies to activate only the functionality they need. This makes the platform flexible and convenient for both end users and implementation partners.

### Deployment Options

iBOX is available as:

- **Cloud solution** — accessible from any device at any time
- **On-premise solution** — available upon request

### Key Features

- Multicurrency support  
- Multi-company management  
- Role-based access control  
- API integrations and customization options  
- Telegram bot for quick mobile interactions

Our goal is to provide businesses with a reliable and user-friendly management system that delivers value from day one.
