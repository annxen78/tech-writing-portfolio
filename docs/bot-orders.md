# 📦 Configuring Orders via Telegram Bot

The System allows you to connect a Telegram bot so that customers can place orders, check stock availability, download price lists, and track shipments — all directly from the messenger.

---

## Navigation

Go to:

**Settings → Organization Settings → Telegram Bot → Customer Bot → Settings**

---

## Access Settings

In the **Who can use this bot?** section, choose one of the following options:

- **All customers** — anyone who opens the bot can access it, even if they are not yet registered in the system  
- **Only system customers** — access is limited to registered customers  
- **Selected customers** — manually choose specific customers from the list  

For more details, see: *Who can use the Telegram bot*

---

## Order Settings

Configure how orders are created via the bot:

- ✅ **Enable** — activates Telegram orders  
- ☑️ **Catalog-only mode** — customers can browse products but cannot place orders  

---

## Product Selection

Click the checkbox next to **Products**.

In the pop-up window:
- select products using search, categories, groups, or suppliers  
- selected items will appear on the right  

Click **Apply**.

---

## Product Display

Choose how products are displayed in the bot:

- by categories, groups, brands, or folders  
- as a grid or list  

---

## Default Settings

These settings are applied when an order is created via the bot:

- **Default price list** — defines product pricing  
- **Default warehouse** — determines where items are reserved from  
- **Default status** — e.g. *Accepted*, *Pending*, *Draft*  

---

## Stock Display Settings

- **Show out-of-stock items** — products remain visible even if unavailable  
- **Show stock levels** — displays available quantity for each item  
- **Show stock across all warehouses** — shows total quantity across all warehouses  

---

## Additional Options

- **Reserve products** — items are automatically reserved when an order is created  
- **Require payment method** — customer must select a payment method  
- **Require delivery address** — customer must enter a delivery address  

---

## Accounts Receivable Report Settings

- ✅ **Enable** — sends a report to the customer  
- **Report type** — by items / by documents / by date  
- **Orientation** — portrait or landscape (PDF only)  
- **Format** — PDF or Excel  
- ☑️ **Include document comments**

---

## Other Features

- ✅ **View last shipment** — allows customers to view their most recent shipment (template can be selected)  
- ✅ **Download price list** — customers can download the latest price list directly from the bot
